What is a transfer?

A vehicle that has a current lease or loan can be transferred to another individual through a transfer process. The Transfer process is conducted by the lending institution that holds the lease or loan. Once a transfer completes the original owner is released from liablity from the loan or lease.

Why transfer a vehicle?

Life is ever changing. Unexpected events such as Divorce, Job Loss, Death or Business Closures can cause massive disruptions without notice. A promotion that comes with a company car, marriage or having a child take over a vehicle from a parent are all examples where transferring a vehicle would be of great benefit.
